There will be three assignments which will improve both your theoretical understanding and your practical skills. All assignments contain programming parts and written questions.

## Deadlines

All assignments are due at 11:59 PM Pacific Time. All deadlines are posted on Ed and on the Schedule page.

## Submission

Assignments are submitted via Gradescope (https://www.gradescope.com/courses/1288404/). Students are automatically added to the course on Gradescope. Use @stanford.edu email for signup.

## Late Policy

See the late policy on the home page:
- 4 free late days per quarter
- Up to 2 late days per assignment
- After exhausting free late days: 25% penalty per additional late day

## Collaboration Policy

- Study groups are allowed and students may discuss in groups.
- Each student must write down solutions independently and hand in one assignment per student.
- List study group member names at the top of your assignment.

### Honor Code
All work submitted must be the student's own. Stanford's Honor Code (https://communitystandards.stanford.edu/policies-and-guidance/honor-code) applies.

### Generative AI Use
Subject to the same policies regarding collaboration. Each student must write solutions independently of AI output. A note denoting the nature of the collaboration should be included. Using generative AI to substantially complete assignments is a violation of the Honor Code.
