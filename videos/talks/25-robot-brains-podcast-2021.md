## Description

Pieter Abbeel interviewed Andrej Karpathy on The Robot Brains Podcast in 2021. The conversation covered Karpathy's journey from academia to industry leadership:

- Karpathy's background: from Stanford PhD and CS231n to OpenAI founding member, then Sr. Director of AI at Tesla
- The progression of Tesla's self-driving AI: from traditional computer vision to end-to-end neural networks
- The concept of "Data Engine" at Tesla — how data collection, labeling, training, and deployment form a continuous feedback loop
- The transition from Software 1.0 (traditional programming) to Software 2.0 (neural networks)
- Karpathy's views on the future of AI, including the trajectory toward general intelligence
- Advice for aspiring AI researchers and engineers

This episode provides a comprehensive overview of Karpathy's career arc and his philosophy on AI development.
