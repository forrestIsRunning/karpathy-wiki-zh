## Description

At the 2024 UC Berkeley AI Hackathon's Awards Ceremony, the atmosphere was electric as Andrej Karpathy, founding member of OpenAI, delivered an inspiring keynote. Out of 371 projects, the top 8 teams took the stage to pitch their groundbreaking AI solutions. After intense deliberation by our esteemed judges, the big reveal came: up to $100K in prizes were awarded, celebrating innovation and creativity in AI for Good. Missed the live ceremony? ## 通俗解释

这是安德烈在加州大学伯克利分校的 AI 编程马拉松（就是一群人在短时间内比赛做项目的活动）上的演讲。来自 371 个团队的学生们做出了各种有创意的 AI 项目，安德烈给大家打气加油。这些项目都是用 AI 来解决现实世界的问题，比如帮助聋哑人交流、保护环境等。最后有 8 支优秀团队上台展示他们的作品，赢得了总共 10 万美元的奖金。
